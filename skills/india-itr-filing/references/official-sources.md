# Official sources and freshness protocol

Check these primary sources for the selected assessment year before relying on a rate, limit, deadline, schema, or eligibility rule.

## Core sources

- Income Tax Department e-Filing portal: https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal/
- Downloads and current utilities: https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal/downloads
- Taxpayer information and acts: https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal/tax-laws-rules
- Income Tax India: https://www.incometaxindia.gov.in/
- Central Board of Direct Taxes: https://incometaxindia.gov.in/Pages/communications/index.aspx

## Useful official guidance

- Form 10-IEA: https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal/help/statutory-forms/popular-form/form-10-IEA-um
- ITR-2 filing guidance: https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal/help/all-topics/e-filing-services/file-itr-2-online
- ITR-4 and presumptive eligibility: https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal/help/all-topics/e-filing-services/file-itr-4-sugam-online
- Old versus new regime FAQs: https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal/help/new-tax-vs-old-tax-regime-faqs
- Foreign-asset guidance: https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal/nudge/nudge-schedule-fa

## Freshness protocol

1. Confirm the assessment year in the portal and on every instruction PDF.
2. Prefer the latest schema, validation rules, utility version, notification, or circular for that year.
3. Record the source URL and access date in working notes.
4. If official sources conflict, follow the later binding notification/circular and flag the conflict.
5. Treat blogs, videos, and forum posts as explanations only; never let them override the Act, Rules, notifications, circulars, form instructions, or current validation schema.
