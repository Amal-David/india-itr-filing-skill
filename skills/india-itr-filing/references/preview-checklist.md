# Final preview checklist

Do not submit until every applicable item is checked.

## Identity and filing

- [ ] Correct assessment year and taxpayer status
- [ ] Correct ITR form and filing section
- [ ] Correct residential status
- [ ] Correct tax regime and Form 10-IEA history
- [ ] Refund bank account is validated and masked in working notes

## Income

- [ ] Salary matches Form 16 after explaining gross-versus-chargeable differences
- [ ] Professional gross receipts reconcile to invoices and bank/remittance records
- [ ] Presumptive income meets the applicable rule
- [ ] Bank interest, dividends, and refund interest are complete
- [ ] House-property status and interest timing are supportable
- [ ] Capital gains match the capital-gains statements, not redemption proceeds
- [ ] Intraday, F&O, and delivery activity are classified separately
- [ ] VDA entries have identifiable source transactions

## Losses and deductions

- [ ] Current-year loss set-off is legally permitted
- [ ] Every brought-forward loss is traceable to a filed earlier return
- [ ] Deductions are allowed under the selected regime and supported by evidence
- [ ] No deduction or expense is claimed twice

## Disclosures

- [ ] Unlisted shares are disclosed with consistent quantities and values
- [ ] Schedule FA matches the applicable calendar-year records
- [ ] Foreign income is offered in the correct schedule when present
- [ ] Foreign-asset answer in Part B-TTI is consistent

## Tax and result

- [ ] TDS/TCS matches Form 26AS and the related income
- [ ] Tax challans match payment records
- [ ] Interest and filing fee are plausible for the filing date
- [ ] Total income and special-rate income are complete
- [ ] Refund or payable result is independently recomputed or plausibility-checked

## Human gate

- [ ] Taxpayer reviewed the preview PDF/JSON
- [ ] All unresolved assumptions are listed
- [ ] Every evidence-register row maps to a preview field or a documented non-reporting conclusion
- [ ] Total income, special-rate income, tax, credits, interest/fee, and refund/payable are recomputed from the supported figures
- [ ] Every material unresolved item is resolved or handed to the appropriate professional
- [ ] Taxpayer personally controls declaration, payment, submission, upload, OTP, and e-verification
