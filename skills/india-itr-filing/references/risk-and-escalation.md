# Risk and escalation

## Risk tiers

- **Green:** ordinary draft assistance with complete evidence, such as one-employer salary or simple domestic investments.
- **Amber:** draft assistance plus focused review, such as multiple employers, an evidence-complete 44ADA candidate, trading with broker reports, property/HRA facts, or a complete VDA ledger.
- **Red:** stop autonomous completion and prepare a professional handoff.

## Mandatory professional review

Use a CA, tax lawyer, RBI/FEMA adviser, or other appropriate professional when any applies:

- residency is uncertain, RNOR/non-resident treatment is disputed, or treaty/foreign-tax credit is involved;
- a notice, defective return, reassessment, condonation, updated-return restriction, or disputed filing position exists;
- books, audit, 44ADA below-floor declaration, derivative turnover method, or material business/trading loss is uncertain;
- prior loss or tax credit cannot be traced to a filed return or official tax record;
- a foreign asset, account, gift, unlisted share, control/director role, signing authority, valuation, ODI/LRS/UIN/AD-bank status, or FEMA classification is uncertain;
- a VDA transfer cannot be identified transaction by transaction;
- property ownership share, possession/completion, construction period, use, co-owner allocation, sale value, or property TDS is uncertain;
- employer, broker, AMC, bank, AIS/TIS, Form 26AS, or portal records remain materially inconsistent;
- the portal cannot represent the supported economic result without a false value.

## Stop-state output

When blocked, do not keep editing the return. Produce:

```text
Blocked issue:
Why it affects form, tax, disclosure, or compliance:
Source records reviewed:
Missing evidence:
Competing treatments:
Official sources checked:
Exact question for the adviser:
Portal fields that must remain unchanged:
```

## Reviewer hierarchy

Resolve conflicts in this order:

1. Act, Rules, binding notifications, and binding judicial authority.
2. Current official form instructions, circulars, and department guidance, each within its legal role.
3. Current AY schema and validation rules for portal mechanics.
4. Primary taxpayer records.
5. Professional technical guidance for computations such as trading turnover.
6. Third-party explanations.

Do not independently “average” conflicting authorities. Record the exact conflict and escalate. Portal behavior is a mechanical constraint, not authority to falsify facts.
