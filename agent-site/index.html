India ITR Filing Agent Skill

A privacy-safe Agent Skill for classifying, preparing, reconciling, reviewing, and troubleshooting Indian individual income-tax returns.

Ways to start

  1. Guided questions for taxpayers who do not know their ITR form.
  2. Known modes such as salary, professional income, investments, trading, property, VDA, or foreign holdings.
  3. Document-led discovery from redacted source records.

The skill assists with filing; it does not replace a Chartered Accountant, advocate, or RBI/FEMA adviser. The taxpayer controls declarations, payments, submission, and verification.
