# Lessons

- Do not overfit a public tax skill to one complex mixed-income case. Start with a short multi-select intake, support simple salary filers as a first-class route, and compose only the evidence packs and schedules that apply.
- Treat modes as workflow routes, not legal form eligibility. Derive the form and regime from current rules and complete evidence.
- A successful portal validation or attractive refund is not proof of completeness; source-to-preview reconciliation remains mandatory.
